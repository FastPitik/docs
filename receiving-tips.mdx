When a runner loves the shot you got of them, they can leave you a **tip** when they buy your photos. It's on top of the ₱125 they pay for the photo, it's completely up to them, and it's added to your normal weekly payout.

<Note>
  Tips are always extra — never instead of your sale. Your photo earnings don't change, and you keep full rights to every photo.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="A runner buys your photos">
    They find their shots by selfie or bib number and check out at ₱125 each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They add a tip (optional)">
    At checkout, the runner can choose to add a tip for their order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The tip goes to you">
    If you were the only photographer, the whole tip is yours. If more than one photographer shot that runner, it's shared — see below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're paid weekly">
    Tips show up in your dashboard right away and pay out on your normal weekly cycle, the same as your photo sales.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The payment fee

<Info>
  Every tip has a **1.5% payment-provider fee** taken off first. This is the cost charged by the payment system, not a Fokus fee — you keep the rest.
</Info>

For example, on a ₱50 tip the fee is ₱0.75, so **₱49.25** goes to you (or is shared, if more than one of you shot the runner).

## When more than one of you shot the same runner

Sometimes a runner buys photos from two or three photographers in one order. When that happens, the tip is shared **based on what the runner actually bought from each of you** — so whoever they bought more from gets the bigger share. It's never divided evenly regardless of who shot what.

Here's an example. A runner buys 3 of your photos and 2 from another photographer, then leaves a ₱50 tip:

|  | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| You sold 3 photos | ₱375 |
| The other photographer sold 2 photos | ₱250 |
| Tip the runner left | ₱50.00 |
| Payment fee (1.5%) | −₱0.75 |
| **Tip to share** | **₱49.25** |
| **Your share** — you sold 3 of the 5 photos | **₱29.55** |
| The other photographer — 2 of the 5 | ₱19.70 |

If you were the only one who shot that runner, the whole ₱49.25 would be yours.

## When you get paid

Tips follow the same schedule as your photo sales. They clear about 48 hours after the sale and pay out every week through InstaPay or PESONet, straight to your bank or e-wallet. You'll see every tip in your dashboard as it comes in.

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do tips change my photo earnings?">
    No. Tips are completely separate and always on top of what you earn from selling your photos. Your per-photo earnings stay exactly the same.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I was the only photographer?">
    The whole tip is yours, minus the 1.5% payment fee.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is the tip shared if several of us shot the runner?">
    By what the runner bought. If they bought more of your photos, you get a bigger share of the tip. Someone who sold fewer photos to that runner gets a smaller share.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to a tip if a photo is refunded?">
    A tip you've already earned is yours to keep — it isn't taken back if a single photo is refunded later.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I still own my photos?">
    Always. Tips don't change anything about your rights. You keep full ownership of every photo and can still sell them elsewhere.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When will I see a tip?">
    Right away. Tips appear in your dashboard the moment a runner leaves one, and they pay out on your next weekly cycle.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
