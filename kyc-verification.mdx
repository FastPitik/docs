Before you can withdraw earnings from Fokus, your account needs to pass identity verification. This protects you and your buyers, and lets us comply with Philippine financial regulations on payouts.

We don't process your personally identifiable information. The verification is handled through our identity partner [**Didit**](https://didit.me). You will need to do the following:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit a valid government‑issued ID">
    Photo of the front (and back, if applicable) of a valid ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a selfie liveness check">
    A short, guided selfie that proves the ID belongs to you and that you're a real person — not a photo of a photo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email address">
    A one‑time link sent to the email address on your Fokus account. Make sure to use the same email you used to signup/login in Fokus.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The whole flow usually takes **about 3 minutes**. Decisions typically come back instantly. In some cases, it might take a few minutes to a few hours to confirm your identity. In rare cases, someone from the Fokus team will reach out to you to complete the verification process.

## **When you need to verify**

You can browse Studio, create collections, and accept sales **without** verifying your identity. You will be prompted to complete verification when:

- You request your first withdrawal, **or**
- A platform admin asks you to (for example, after an unusual sales pattern).

<Note>
  Once verified, you don't need to repeat the flow unless your verification expires (typically after 2 years) or you change the legal name on your account.
</Note>

## **What you'll need before you start**

<Warning>
  Have these ready before you begin — once you start the flow, you have ~10 minutes to finish before the session expires.
</Warning>

<Card title="A valid government‑issued ID" icon="id-card">
  Philippine passport, driver's license, UMID, National ID, Residence Permit. Must be unexpired and clearly readable. SSS or TIN cards alone are not accepted.
</Card>

<Card title="Decent lighting" icon="lightbulb">
  Avoid glare on the ID. Plain background works best for the selfie.
</Card>

<Card title="A working camera" icon="camera">
  Phone or laptop. Front-facing camera is fine. You'll be guided through positioning.
</Card>

<Card title="Access to your email" icon="envelope-circle-check">
  The email on your Fokus account. You'll need to submit the OTP sent in your inbox to complete the verification process.
</Card>

## **The verification flow, step by step**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your ID type and capture the front">
    Select the ID you'll use from the list. Hold it flat in good light. Follow the on‑screen guide that frames where to place the ID. The app captures automatically once everything's aligned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture the back, if applicable">
    Driver's licenses, UMID, and PhilSys IDs need both sides. Passports don't.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the selfie liveness check">
    Look at the camera, then follow the prompts — typically turning your head slowly side to side and/or blinking. This proves you're a live person, not a held‑up photo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Open the email titled "Verify your Fokus account" and click the link inside. The link is single-use and expires after 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the decision">
    Most decisions land within a few minutes; complex reviews can take a few hours. Rest assured when you're approved, you can then request withdrawals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **If verification fails**

The most common reasons:

| **Reason** | **What to do** |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Blurry ID photo** | Retake in better light, hold the ID flatter, and make sure no fingers cover important fields. |
| **Glare washing out the ID** | Tilt the ID slightly away from the light source, or move to softer lighting. |
| **Expired ID** | Use a different valid ID, or renew the document and try again. |
| **Selfie doesn't match the ID** | Look directly at the camera, remove glasses or hats if possible, and make sure your face is well-lit. |
| **Liveness check fails repeatedly** | Try from a device with a better camera; a phone usually works better than a low-resolution webcam. |
| **Wrong email** | Check **Settings → Account** for the email on file. If it's wrong, update to use the email used in Fokus, then restart verification. |

## **Privacy and what we store**

<Note>
  Fokus does **not** store copies of your ID images or your selfie. Didit holds these securely on our behalf and provides us only with the verification decision and the personal data you'd see on the ID (legal name, date of birth, ID number).
</Note>

What Fokus retains after a verification decision:

- Your **verified legal name and date of birth** — used to populate payout records and TIN reporting if required.
- The **decision** (verified, rejected, expired) and its date.
- The **type of ID** used (not the ID number itself, unless required by the payout institution).
- A **reference token** that lets us re-query Didit if needed.

What gets deleted at Didit after the decision is final:

- The ID images you submitted.
- The selfie liveness frames.
- Any biometric data extracted during the check.

For more detail, see Didit's ([https://didit.me/privacy](https://didit.me/privacy)) and our own ([https://fokus.ph/privacy](https://fokus.ph/privacy)).
